Privacy Policy — Orbit Rush

Last updated: May 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Orbit Rush handles your data. By playing Orbit Rush, you agree to this policy.

1. Data We Collect

Orbit Rush itself does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The game runs in your browser and does not require registration or login.

However, Orbit Rush uses Google AdMob to display rewarded advertisements. AdMob may collect the following data to serve relevant ads:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable device identifier)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

2. How This Data Is Used

Data collected by AdMob is used solely for the purpose of serving and measuring advertisements. Orbit Rush does not have access to this data and does not use it for any other purpose.

3. Google AdMob

Orbit Rush uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC. AdMob's data collection and use is governed by Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

https://www.google.com/settings/ads

4. Children's Privacy

Orbit Rush is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
